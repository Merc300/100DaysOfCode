About

Our moon is truly incredible! Not only does it help our planet balance on axis, it also helps provide stability to our climate. It is is known by NASA as the the Earth's "natural satellite".

A Few Orbital Facts

  • Average distance from Earth: 238,855 miles or 384,400 km
  • Closest approach to Earth (perigee): 225,700 miles or 363,300 km
  • Farthest distance from Earth (apogee): 252,000 miles or 405,500 km
